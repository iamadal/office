Dictionary: sb = somebody
            sth = Something            
01. Two Parts
    Subject = যার সম্পর্কে বা যাকে উদ্দ্যেশ্য করে(What/who)
    Predicate = কর্তা সম্পর্কে যা বলা হয়
02. Subject(based on Nour/Pronoun) 
    Predicate(based on Verb)
    
    Special Case: (You) Come here কর্তা উহ্য
                  (You) Please do it
03. Complement: কর্তার একটি নতুন পরিচয়। ('to be verb' এর পর যেসব শব্দ যুক্ত হয়)
    He is(to be) -- a Teacher(করিম) -- 
                
04. Subject এর অবস্থান: 
     বাক্যের শুরুতে: *He* is a Teacher
     বাক্যের শেষে : Long live *Bangladesh*
05. বিস্তৃতি: 
  as noun      : *Karim* is my brother
  as adjective : *The virtuous* are happy.
  as pronoun   : *He* is a good boy
  as gerund    : *Smoking* is a bad habit
  as phrase    : *To walk* is good
  as clause    : *what he says* is right

 01. Assertive(বিবৃতি) : I eat rice
     a. Affirmative(হ্যা বোধক): I know him.
        Structure: Sub+v+..........
     
     b. Negative(না বোধক): I do not know him.
        Sub+ do not/does not + main verb+...
        
     c. Emphatic(জোরালো বিবৃতি): I do like him. 
     
02. Interrogative(প্রশ্নবোধক): What is your name?
    বৈশিষ্ট্য:
    a. Start with Aux. or modal verb.(ans. yes/no)
    
    aux./modal+sub+mv+.....
    Are(aux.) you(sub) ready(mv) to go?
 
    
    b. Start with Wh-words: 3 Forms
    কে = subject
    কি = Object
       1. Interrogative pronoun
          a. Who(sub/pro.) discovered america?
          কে আমেরিকা আবিষ্কার করেছিলো?
          b. What(sub/pro.) made you sad?
          কে তোমার মন খারাপ করেছে?
          c. What(obj) do you like?
          তুমি কি পছন্দ কর?
          
      2. Form: adj+Noun
         What(adj) colour(nour) do you like?
         which book(n) shall i get?
         
      3. Interrogative adverb of time
         When are you coming from?
         why are you so late?

    How(adverb): কিভাবে/কেমন/কত/কেমন করে/কি উপায়ে
                 কি অবস্থায়/কেমন করে/
    Examples:
    আচরণ:
    a. How does it work.
    b. He does not know how to swim.
    c. I'll show you how to load files.
    d. Her behaviour was very odd."how so?"
    e. it's funny how(that) people remember him.
    
    স্বাস্থ্য:
    a. How are you?
    b. How are you feeling now?
    
    অন্যান্য:(Before adverb/Adjective)
    a. How was your trip?(enjoy)
    b. How did they play?(success/win or lose)
    c. I didn't know how much to bring(amount)
    d. How old is she?(age) 
    e. How many people were there? (degree)
    f. How much are those earring?
    g. How kind of you to help!(Surprise)
    h. How he wished he had been there(pleasure)
        
      
         
      
      
    Who  : কে/যে
    Examples:
    নাম/পরিচয় জানা:
    a. Who are you?(identity)
    b. I wonder who that letter was from.
    c. Who are you phoning?(People)
    d. Who is the money for?
    e. The people (who) we met have sent us a card.
    f. John Nash, Who has a lot of experience(info)
    
    Special Case: Who's Who (names/job status)
    ** You'll soon find out who's who in the office
    
    
    Which: কোনটি/যেটি(pro)./যাহা(adj.)
    a. Which is better exercise-swimming od tennis?
    b. Which of the Applicants has got the job?
    c. Which of the patients have recovered?
    d. Which way is the wind blowing?
    e. Houses which overlook the lake cost more.
    f. His best movie, which won several awards.
    
    What(pro. determiner) : কি/কি জিনিস/কি কি/যা তা/
    Examples:
    a. What(=what job) does he do?
    b. What is your name?
    c. What time is it?
    d. What kind of music do you like?
    
    e. What you need is a good meal.
    f. Nobody knows what will happen next?
    g. What a beautiful bird!
    
    IDM:
    and what not | and what have you
    h. it is full of old toys, books and what not.
    i. I'll give her what for if she does that again
    j. Is he stupid or what?
    k. Are you going now or what?
    l. What? I can't hear you.
    m. 'Mummy!' 'What?''I'm thirsty
    n. 'I ask her to merry me.''You what?'(Surprise)
    o. it will cost $500,'What?' (anger)
    p. What about you,Joe? do you like it?(introduce)
    q. What about a trip to France?(Suggestion)
    r. She is just gone out old what-d'you-call-him
    s. What is this tool for?(Reason)
    t. What(=why) did you do that?
    u. What if the train is late?
    v. Yes, I wrote the article. What of it?
    w. They dropped their best player. What'up?
    x. What's with you? You haven't said a word
       all morning.(Strange behave)
    y. What is with all this walking? Can't we take

    Whom(pronoun): যাদের/কাহাকে/যাহাদিগকে/কাহাদিগকে
    Example: used instead of 'who' as the object
             of a verb or preposition
     a. To whom Should i write?
     b. Whom did they invite?
     c. The author whom you criticized in your 
         review has written a replay.
     d. He was free to marry whomever he chose
     
     Whose: কার/যার/কার অন্তরগত?
     a. Whose house is that?
     b. I wonder whose that is.
     c. He is a man whose opinion i respect.
     d. it's the house whose door is painted red.
     e. Isobel, Whose brother he was, had heard 
        the joke before.
     
     N.B ever(adverb) = কখনো = at any time
     Used in negative sentence.
     
     a. Nothing ever happen here
     b. Don't you ever get tired?
     c. if you're ever in miami, see us
     d. Have you ever thought of changing your job?
     e. Have you ever been to  rome?
     f. She hardly ever(=almost never) goes out.
     g. I'll never ever do that again.
     
     h. It was raining harder than ever.(Emphasis)
     i. It's my best ever score(compare)
    
     use to mean always
     
     a. Paul, ever the optimist, agreed to try 
        again.
     b. They lived happily ever after
     c. He would love her for ever
     d. The ever-growing problem
     e. an ever present danger.
     
** used after why, when to show that you are 
   surprised
     a. Why ever did you agree?
     
     b. All he ever does is grumble about things.   
       (Emphasis)
       
     c. He's had a car ever since he was 18 
       (Continuously since)
     
     d. He looks ever so smart(very)
     e. It was ever so easy. 
     f. was/is/does+ever(emphasis)
        You must have been upset by that'Was i 
        ever'
      
     Where(adverb): কোথায়/যেথায়/
  ** Place/situation
     a. Where do you live?
     b. Where(=at what point) did i go wrong in my
        calculation.
     c. Where(=book/news) did you read that?
     d. Just Where(=to what situation) is all this 
        leading us?
     
     as conjunction
     a. This is where I live.
     b. Sit Where I can see you.
     c. That is where(=the point) your are wrong
     
     Special Forms:
     whereabouts(noun/adverb)
     ** uncountable+Sin/Plu. Verb
    * His whereabouts are/is still unknown.
    * Whereabouts did you find it?
    
    
    Where.as
    a. Some of the studies show positive results, 
       where as other do not(Compare)
    
    Where.by = যারা দ্বারা
    a. They have introduced a new system whereby
       all employee must undergo regular training.
       
     Where.in = in which place or thing
     a. an organization wherein each employee is 
        valued and respected.
        
     Where.of = of what or which
     a. I know whereof I speak(= I know well)
     
     Where.upon = as a result of this/and then
     
     He told her she was a liar,where upon(and then
    )  she walked out.
    
    
    Wher-ever = any place
    Sit Wherever you want
    
    Where-withal = the money or things that you 
    need to do something
    
    a. They lacked the wherewithal to pay the 
       repair.
       
       
      When=(কখন/যখন)  (adverb/pronoun/conj.)
      
      adverb:
        Time:
       a. When did you last see him?
       b. When can i see you?
       
        expression of time(=at which)
       a. Sunday is the only day when i eat
       b. The last time i went to scotland was in

       pronoun
       a. Until When can you stay?
       b. I have got a new job.'Since when?'
       
       Conjunction

  b. Call me when(after) you have finished
  c. He had just drifted off to sleep when(just
  after) the phone rang
  d. She claimed to be 18 when(although) I know she
     is 16
     
     
     Special Case:
     Whence(from where)
     
     They returned whence they had come.
     
     Whenever(Conjunction)

     b. Whenever(everytime) She comes, she bring a
        friend.

      Whenever=when used as 
      
      
      Why(কেনো/কি কারনে/কি উদ্দ্যেশ্য)

বাক্য(order,command,advise,reqiest,proposal) প্রকাশ
NB. Second person a you উহ্য থাকবে। তবে জোরালো ভাবে 
করতে বল্লে you hoba.
    a. Don't do it(You do not do it)
    b. You do it at once(জোরালো)
    
 Rules: Subject এর অবজেক্ট ফর্ম+ ইনফিনিটিভ এর to উহ্য
     01. Let+ (1st/3rd) Person 
         Let him do(=to do) the work.
         Let me go home
     02. Proposal/Suggestion
         Let us give thanks to allah.
     03. অনরোধে Please/kindly(Please শেষে দিলে কমা 
         দিতে হবে)
     04. অনুনয়(would you, will you, could you)
         Would you mind+v+ing
         Would you please+base form
         
     05. Do বসিয়ে জোর দেয়া যায়।
         Do hurry up.

             According to structure
---------------------------------------------------
01. Simple: One Sub+One Finite Verb
            I am playing in the field.
            I eat rice.
            
02. Complex
    ১টি প্রিন্সিপাল ক্লোজ+একাধিক সাব অরডিনেট ক্লোজ
    I was so weak that i cannot walk
    
03. Compound:
    স্বতন্ত্র প্রিন্সিপাল ক্লোজ গুলি and,but,or,nor,also
    however,morever,thus,so,therefore,else,still
    as well as, accordingly, otherwise, yet, while
   ,  whereas, not only, but also
   N.B: দুটি ক্লোজে একই সাবজেক্ট হলে পরেরটি উহ্য থাকবে।
   I went to market and (I) bought a pen.
   
   
   Complex এবং Compound এর পার্থক্য: Compound এর প্রতিটি ক্লোজ স্বাধীন এবং স্বতন্ত্র অর্থ থাকে যা Complex এ নাই

---------------------------------------------------But:
Conj: কিন্তু, তবে, তা,  বিনা



01. However/Dispite/but then
    * We were tired but(however) happy
02. Disagree/Surprise.
    * But that not possible.
03. Except/apart from
    * I had no choice but to sing it.
04. if it were not for
    * He would have played but for a knee injury
05. You cannot but.
    * What could he do but forgive her?
    
prep: ছাড়া,  ব্যতিত(but = except)

adv : অথচ, কেবল মাত্র, সবে

    but(only) try.

or 
conj: বা/অথবা/না/নচেৎ

01. Possibility
    * is it a boy or girl?
02. used in negative sentences
    * I can't read or write(can't write)
03. Else(অন্যথায়)
    * Turn the heat down or it'll burn. 
    * He must like her,or He would not keep calling
04. approximation
    * We were six or seven members.
05. Similarity
    * Geology, or the science of the earth's crust.
06. Introduce a constrasting idea.
    * He was lying- or was he?
07. Or so(about)

 * Not a Building nor(and not) a tree was left.
