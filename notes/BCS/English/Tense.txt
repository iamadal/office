01. Present Tense
    a. Indefinite: Base form of Verb
    b. Continuous: v+ing (am/is/are) তেছি তেছো তেছেন
    c. Perfect: v PP form,have/has ইয়াছি, ইয়াছ
    d. Perfect Continuous: has/have been v+ing
    
02. Past Tense
    a. Indefinite: Base form of Verb
    b. Continuous: v+ing (am/is/are) তেছি তেছো তেছেন
    c. Perfect: v PP form,have/has ইয়াছি, ইয়াছ
    d. Perfect Continuous: has/have been v+ing
    
03. Future Tense
    a. Indefinite: shall/will+V base form
    b. Continuous: v+ing (am/is/are) তেছি তেছো তেছেন
    c. Perfect: v PP form,have/has ইয়াছি, ইয়াছ
    d. Perfect Continuous: has/have been v+ing
