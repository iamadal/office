উদ্দ্যেশ্য: Noun/Pronoun/Noun Phrase এর অাগে বসে বাক্যের অন্য কোন শব্দের সহিত সম্পর্ক স্থাপন করে। যার অাগে বসবে তাকে Prepositional Object বলে।


Types of Preposition.
01. Simple : At, on, by, for,  from

02. Double: into(in+to), within(with+in)

03. Compound: Noun/Adjective/Adverb + Simple Preposition.
Structure: 
replace on > a
               by > b/be
Example: across = on+cross
                  about. = on+by+out
                  behind= by+hind
                  before = by+fore
                  above = on+by+up
                  below = by+low
                  along = on+long
                  between = by+twin
                  beside = by + side
                  beyond = by+yond

04. Phrase:
on account of, by dint of, by means of, in front of, in stead of, look for,because of, in lieu of, as for, in spite of, in front of
প্রিপজিশন দিয়ে শুরু প্রিপজিশন দ্বারা সেষ

05. Participle: Present(v+ing) or Past Participle(v এর PP Form) used as preposition
According, assuming, concerning, regarding, speaking

06. Disguised:
if a or o is used in lieu of on, at, of.

সংক্ষিপ্ত: S C DD PP

E.G: it is six o clock(o-of)
He went a hunting(on)


বিচ্ছিন্ন(Detached)
Where has he come (from)?
অর্থাৎ প্রশ্নবোধক বাক্যে Noun/Pronoun হতে বিচ্ছিন্ন তাই এমন নাম। এক্ষেত্রে শেষে বসবে একদম। Where are you come from?

Interrogative pronoun,  Relative pronoun, adverb এর সহিত ব্যবহূত হয়।

যথা:
This is the orphan(whom) we called for.
There is no truth in what you say. 




Other Parts of Speech as Preposition
------------------------------------
Noun: (Despite) his Failure, He did not lose heart.

Adjective: The School is situated (near) the market

Verb: All attended the picnic (save) your brother.

Participle: We Shall reach there before half (past) nine

conjunction: None(but) Rahim has done it.
they are called Special Preposition.
